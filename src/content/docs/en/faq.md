# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Atoms.

## General

### What is Atoms?

Atoms is an AI-powered code generation platform that uses multiple AI agents to plan, code, and review web applications based on your natural language descriptions.

### Is my code saved?

Yes. All projects, chat history, and generated files are saved to your account and persist across sessions.

### What languages/frameworks are supported?

Currently, Atoms generates HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The generated code can use React (via CDN) and other popular frontend libraries.

## Account

### How do I create an account?

Visit the login page and click "Sign Up". Enter your email and a password (minimum 6 characters).

### Can I delete my account?

Contact support to request account deletion. All your projects and data will be permanently removed.

## AI & Generation

### Which AI models are available?

- **Claude** by Anthropic (default)
- **GPT-4** by OpenAI

You can switch models in project settings.

### How does the multi-agent system work?

When you send a request:

1. **Planner Agent** analyzes your requirements and creates a technical plan
2. **Coder Agent** generates complete code based on the plan
3. **Reviewer Agent** reviews the code for quality and suggests improvements

### Can I iterate on generated code?

Yes! Continue chatting in the workspace to refine and modify your application. The AI has context of your full conversation history.

### Why is generation slow sometimes?

Generation speed depends on:
- Complexity of your request
- Current AI model load
- Network conditions

> **Tip**: Simpler, more focused requests generate faster.
